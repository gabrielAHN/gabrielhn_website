Bio Now Returning to NYC Albania

Now

I am getting more familiar with the inner workings and political life within NYC through my work at the Taxi and Limousine Commission on the External Affairs Team. I have been able to be part of the political and industry side of the NYC Taxi industry, which recently has been experiencing extreme shifts because of emerging technology. Through this exposure, I deepened my knowledge of the relationships between government, technology, and communities.

Also, in my free time I am working on side projects that are more coding centered to create data collection systems and analysis of that data to better understanding technologies role within city design and policies. Check out my recent work here

Returning to NYC

Returning back from Albania, I immediately returned to work. I came back on a Wednesday and immediately started working on Friday at the Greenwich Village Society for Historic Preservation (GVSHP) in NYC. That quick turnaround eased me into the transition back into life, and introduced me to architecture, organized community activism, and NYC politics. GVSHP taught me about the inner workings of cities, and helped me build personal connections to the NYC communities GVSHP was active in.

However, I joined to pursuit my interest in architecture, but as I learned more about the field I found it was not for me so I left to do freelance work, learn more skills, and grow my network.

During these times I was starting personal projects like NJ Latino Historias, and POPS Programs Redesigns.
