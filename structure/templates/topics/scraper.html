Christie's Auction House Data Scrape Analysis

The project started from a friend who told me about her analysis of art to make a financial model for their prices. This sparked me to see for myself if there were different criteria the could determine the price of pieces of art. I choose Christie's Auction as the focus of this project. Initial BS4 Data Scrape and Graphs

To start the analysis, I did an initial scrape of Christie's Website. I created a python script using BS4 package that scraped all the auctions that Christie's posted on their website from 1998 to 2017. Click here to see the Webscraping Python Script. From the data collected, I cleaned it and created the graphs below that show trends in Christie's auction houses relating to location and the total sum of the items each locations sold.
