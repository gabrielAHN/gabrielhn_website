Dock Usage Map

I created a folium map that shows the distribution of when a trip starts and ends at each dock through colors to better spot the difference between the amounts, and also radius size for the amount of trips. Included in this map are also the top 10 used Citi bike docks and two layers for trips started and ended at each station. Explore all this data below:
Map Full Screen
Legend

  • Majority of ended bike trips at the Dock
  • Top Ten Used Citi Bike Dock
Note: I colored differentiated the major usages of each dock by a dock having more than 55% of either end or start total trips. Additionally, I determined the radius size of each circle by dividing each corresponding dock count by the max dock amount of the layer it was part of.
